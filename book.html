The Monkey
With One Hand

By Fortune Okunloye  ·  D'Marie Vivre Learning Hub

A young monkey longs to explore the world and ventures beyond the boundaries his family has set. Through vivid illustrations and warm storytelling, children discover the meaning of love, protection, and why those who truly care for us set boundaries — not to cage us, but to keep us safe.

Why boundaries are a form of love, not restriction
Understanding the gentle power of consequences
The importance of listening to those who protect us
Resilience and healing after a difficult lesson
