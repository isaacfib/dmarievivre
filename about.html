Who is Ms. Mary?

Mary Okunloye — known warmly to learners and families as Ms. Mary — is the founder and lead facilitator of D'Marie Vivre Learning Hub. She is an experienced educator, curriculum developer, published children's book author, and a deeply passionate advocate for holistic education.

Her journey into education was shaped by a single conviction: that every child carries within them a unique light. The right environment, the right approach, and a caring educator can help that light shine brightly. That conviction became D'Marie Vivre.

The Hub's Story

1

The Vision

Ms. Mary saw a gap — children who needed more than a classroom, and families who lacked the resources to bridge it. She set out to change that.

2

The Foundation

D'Marie Vivre Learning Hub opened its doors at 139, Trademore Avenue, Lugbe, Abuja — a physical space built on three words: Learn. Grow. Thrive.

3

The Book

"The Monkey With One Hand" was published — a children's book by Fortune Okunloye, emerging from the hub's storytelling culture and moral-centred approach.

4

2025 — Expanded

Saturday Tech Club launched. Special Needs Education deepened. The hub grew to serve children from 2-year-olds in story circles through to secondary school learners.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values &
Our Approach

Creativity

We believe every learner has creative potential. Our activity-based approach sparks imagination at every level.

Diligence

Consistent effort over time builds mastery. We model this in our teaching and expect it of ourselves first.

Nurturing

We see the whole person — safety, belonging, and emotional wellbeing matter as much as academic progress.

Growth

Progress over perfection. Every step forward is celebrated. Growth never has an age limit.
