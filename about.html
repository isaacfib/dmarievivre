Who is Ms. Mary?

Mary Okunloye — known warmly to learners and families as Ms. Mary — is the founder and lead facilitator of D'Marie Vivre Learning Hub. She is an experienced educator, curriculum developer, published children's book author, and a deeply passionate advocate for holistic education.

Her journey into education was shaped by a single conviction: that every child — and every adult — carries within them a unique light. The right environment, the right approach, and a caring educator can help that light shine brightly. That conviction became D'Marie Vivre.

The Hub's Story

1

The Vision

Ms. Mary saw a gap — children who needed more than just a classroom, and adults who had been told learning had an age limit. She disagreed with both.

2

The Foundation

D'Marie Vivre Learning Hub opened its doors at 139, Trademore Avenue, Lugbe, Abuja — a physical space built on three words: Learn. Grow. Thrive.

3

The Book

"The Monkey With One Hand" was published — a children's book written by Fortune Okunloye, emerging from the hub's storytelling culture and moral-centred approach.

4

2025 — Expanded

Adult Education launched. Saturday Tech Club began. The hub expanded to serve everyone from 2-year-olds in story circles to adults learning to read for the first time.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values &
Our Approach

🎨

Creativity

We believe every learner has creative potential. Our activity-based approach sparks imagination at every level.

💪

Diligence

Consistent effort over time builds mastery. We model this in our teaching and expect it of ourselves first.

🤲

Nurturing

We see the whole person — safety, belonging, and emotional wellbeing matter as much as academic progress.

🌱

Growth

Progress over perfection. Every step forward is celebrated. Growth never has an age limit.
