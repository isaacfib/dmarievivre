What is KidsPray?

Faith woven into every
part of who we are

KidsPray is not a separate brand — it's the heartbeat of D'Marie Vivre. Prayer, scripture, and faith are woven throughout every program, resource, and celebration we offer.

Daily Prayer Habits

We help children build consistent, meaningful prayer routines through structured prayer packs, morning devotionals, and scripture affirmations. Faith is a daily practice — not just Sundays.

Birthday Blessings

Our KidsPray birthday surprise packages celebrate your child with prayer, purpose, and the Word of God. Because birthdays are more than cake — they are covenant moments.

Parent & Teacher Tools

Prayer flows through the parents and teachers who lead children. We equip families with tools to model prayer and speak life over the children they love every single day.
