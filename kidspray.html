What is KidsPray?

Faith woven into every
part of who we are

KidsPray is not a separate brand — it's the heartbeat of D'Marie Vivre. Prayer, scripture, and faith are woven throughout every program, resource, and celebration we offer.

📖

Daily Prayer Habits

We help children build consistent, meaningful prayer routines through structured prayer packs, morning devotionals, and scripture affirmations. Faith is a daily practice — not just Sundays.

🎂

Birthday Blessings

Our KidsPray birthday surprise packages are a way to celebrate your child with prayer, purpose, and the Word of God. Because birthdays are more than cake — they are covenant moments.

🤝

Parent & Teacher Tools

Prayer doesn't only happen with the child — it flows through the parents and teachers who lead them. We equip adults with tools to model and pray over the children they love.
